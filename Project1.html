Welcome to Bunch's Book Trade! This website aims to connect like-minded individuals, eager to share their favorite titles and authors via anonymous trading. To get started, check out the "How it Works" section.

About Us:

Bunch's Book Trade started as a passion project of mine. Since the time I learned how, I've always loved to read. I'm also a firm believer that variety is the spice of life. I love meeting new people and partaking in new experiences. So, I came to the conclusion that one of the best ways to steep myself in the variety of life is to experience the stories that my peers love the most. So, I started asking people: "What's your favorite book?" I would read whatever they answered with. While I didn't enjoy every book at first, I could at least appreciate why my classmates and coworkers did themselves. The best part was the conversations the endeavor led to. Eventually, though, I ran out of peers to ask and moved to online forums. From there, the progression to standalone website was all but inevitable. Today, I still partake in trading and reading these books, gaining more appreciation for every new experience along the way. I hope you find as much joy and wonder as I have connecting with people through these works.

How it Works:

Here at Bunch's Book Trade, we like to give you options.
The easiest way to get involved is this: Donate any book -- preferably, your favorite book -- by mail or at one of our drop-off locations (See our "Donate" tab ). By default, donations made will be accepted as just that: donations. If you wish to receive a book in return, follow these instructions:

  1. Include a message with your donation indicating you'd like to join our trade program.
    (Physical note by mail, digital note when donating online.)
  2. Allow 3-5 business days for us to contact you with confirmation of receiving your donation.
  3. Once we contact you, confirm your name and address so we can add you to our trade system.
    This is also the time to tell us your book preferences.
  4. Allow 10-15 business days to receive your new book.
  5. Enjoy your new book for as long as you like -- we'll keep track of the details.
  6. When you're done with your book, notify us online. We'll provide instructions to
    send it back so that you can receive a new one!
That's it! Our trade system is powered entirely by your donations. Feel free to send as many books as you like, repeating the same process if you'd like to receive a book for each donation. Currently, we limit members to 10 books in circulation at a time.

Enjoy your reading adventure!
